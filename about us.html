Grab N' Go 2019 Online Fast Food, CT

Grab N' Go was opened in 2019 and was founded with only php 20000. Grab N' Go experienced rapid growth, it was able to withstand the entry of McDonald's and Jollibee in the Philippines. Then, to produce huge quantities of food at low prices, the co-founders decided to achieve do it on social platforms. This included a self-service that eliminated the need for waiters/waitresses and customers received their food quickly as possible. By focusing the specific tastes of the fast food market which different to other fast food chain.

Grab N' Go aims to provide easy access when purchasing fast food through the use of social platforms, including an online shop. Grab N' Go will continue to expand domestically and internationally, emphasizing affordable food, fun, and flavours that appealed to children and adults alike.
